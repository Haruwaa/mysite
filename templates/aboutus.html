About GOseniorhigh

GOseniorhigh is a website that tests user's personality, interests and characteristics to recommend senior highschool strand for the user who take the test. GOseniorhigh collaborate with other user and recommend strand based on their behavior similarity

Our start

GOseniorhigh was developed by a group of computer science students of Laguna State Polytechnic University and aims to help undecided upcoming senior highschool students to choose their career track by evaluating their personality and interest in a test.
